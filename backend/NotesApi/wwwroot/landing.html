Features

What it does

Handwriting OCR

Draw or upload handwriting and the app reads it back as text, with a confidence score for each word. Works with print or cursive.

Summaries

When you save a note, the app writes a short summary of it, so you can see what it's about without rereading the whole thing.

#Hashtag Filters

Add #hashtags as you write. Click a tag to see every note that uses it.

PDF & CSV Export

Export any note as a PDF or CSV file. Download your notes whenever you want.
